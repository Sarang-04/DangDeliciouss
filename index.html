    About The Chef

    Hi, I’m Sidra! Being in the kitchen and cooking has always been something I love — it’s my happy place where creativity comes alive. I believe that every dish tells a story, and for me, food isn’t just about taste; it’s about creating art on a plate. It’s about the process. The calm that comes with chopping, mixing, waiting — all of it.

    My first kitchen experiment was when I was little. It was a simple bread and cheese pizza. It was far from perfect — actually, kind of a mess. But I remember how much everyone appreciated it anyway. That small moment of encouragement meant everything to me. It made me want to try again, learn more, and keep going. What started as a one-time experiment slowly became something I genuinely loved doing.

    Even though cooking has always been a part of my life, Dang Delicious, my blog — officially started in 2020. Here, I’ll be sharing all my favorite recipes straight from my kitchen to yours.
